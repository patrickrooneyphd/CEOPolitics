You have been asked to participate in an economic experiment in which you may earn a significant amount of money. Please read these instructions carefully, since you will only see this page once.

The experiment consists of a set of strategic decision-making tasks on your computer. Please complete this experiment on a desktop or laptop, not a mobile device. Your identity and the decisions you make will remain anonymous to the researchers and any other participants in this experiment.

In this experiment, you will be assuming the role of CEO in a small sweater company. You are in charge of making strategic decisions on a new line of sweater. There are four strategic decisions to make:

  • Type (cardigan, sweater vest, or turtleneck)
  • Cotton content in the sweater (0% - 100%)
  • Price of the sweater ($9 - $109)
  • Social engagement with a non-profit cause
As part of this last decision, you will make choices on how to strategically engage with {{ cause }}, {{ cause_statement }}. Your engagement can take one of two forms: taking no social action or donating 10% of your earnings in a period to {{ cause }}.

The previous CEO of your company has left you a letter detailing the company's historic approach to strategic decision-making. You will see this letter on the next page; please read it carefully since you will not be able to return to this page.

This experiment consists of 20 periods split into two halves (periods 1 - 10 in the first half, and periods 11 - 20 in the second half). In each period, you will make strategic decisions about sweater production and learn the earnings associated with each decision set. Following these 20 periods, you will complete a short questionnaire and will be informed of your earnings. One period from each half will be chosen at random at the end of the experiment for payment, and the computer will average these two values for your final payment. The page will also note any earnings you chose to donate to {{ cause }}.

There is a good chance you will make more than 6.75 USD. Any amount you earn over 6.75 USD will be paid to you as a bonus within 5 business days of completing the experiment.

All earnings and donations in this experiment are paid out in real money. You will receive your earnings after participating in the experiment, and {{ cause }} will receive the accumulated donations from participants once all experimental sessions are complete.

This experiment should take around 45 minutes to complete. Before beginning, please carefully read the following statement.


NOTE: This experiment requires the informed consent of participants. This survey is part of an academic project conducted by researchers at the University of Toronto. Your identity will remain anonymous, and any data gathered will not be identifiable. You may withdraw from this experiment at any time by exiting your browser. If you choose to withdraw, your data will not be retained and will be immediately destroyed. If you have any questions about this study, please contact the research supervisor, Nicola Lacetera, at nicola.lacetera@utoronto.ca. If you have concerns about any harmful ethical implications of this research, please reach out to ethics@utoronto.ca.
