As a final note, your decisions will have effects on the company's reputation. Given the company's lack of social action in the past, donating 10% of earnings to {{ cause }} may anger some of your customers, resulting in lower payoffs if you choose to switch back to taking no social action. Specifically, when you play a strategy in which you do not donate, you will incur a penalty of ($0.50 x number of previous periods in which you donated). If you play a strategy in which you donate, you will not be subject to this penalty. If you never donate, you will not be subject to this penalty. You cannot earn less than $0.00 in any period.

As an example, suppose you choose to donate 10% to {{ cause }} in the first three periods as part of your strategy. In the third period, suppose your strategy earns you $3.60 and generates $0.40 in donations. For the fourth period, suppose you choose to play the previous CEO's strategy (and not donate). Your fourth period earnings will be $3.00 - $1.50 = $1.50, where $3.00 is the original payoff that you would earn from the previous CEO's strategy minus a $0.50 penalty for each of the three previous rounds in which you donated. If instead you had chosen to play the same strategy in the fourth period as you did in the third period, you would receive the original (non-penalized) payoff for that strategy, $3.60 and a $0.40 donation.
